Publishing on another MobileMe account

If you have access to more than one MobileMe account, e.g. if you have several account names as part of a Family Pack, you may wish to publish your Sandvox site to an account that is different from the one set in System Preferences.

To do so, you will need to enter your account information manually by choosing the option to publish to a remote host.

On the Publishing Locations screen, choose "FTP/SFTP/WebDAV".

Enter the following information:

URL Format
http://web.me.com/account/
Hostname
idisk.mac.com
Upload Method
WebDAV

Enter the account ID and password for the account.

Document Root
/account/Web/Sites   (note the "/" at the beginning)

Enter a subfolder if you are planning on putting more than one website on this MobileMe account.
