Deleting a pagelet

To delete a pagelet:

  1. Click the pagelet to select it.
  2. A warning may appear informing you that the pagelet cannot be selected. If so, click "Select" and click the pagelet again.
  3. Choose "Delete Pagelet" from the Edit menu.

You can also control-click the pagelet and choose "Delete Pagelet" or press command-option-delete.

You can undo this by choosing "Undo Delete Pagelet" from the Edit menu.
