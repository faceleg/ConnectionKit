Editing Marker

An Editing Marker () appears wherever you can insert text. Generally this is used to give a pagelet a caption.

Click the Editing Marker to insert some text and begin editing it. To remove the text again, simply delete all of it.

Editing Markers are only present while you edit a site in Sandvox. When the site is published, visitors do not see the markers. While editing the site, you can temporarily hide or show Editing Markers.

Ways to hide or show Editing Markers:

  • Click the "Hide/Show Markers" item in the toolbar.
  • Choose "Hide/Show Editing Markers" from the View menu.
