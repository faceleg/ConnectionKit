Main content area


Each page contains multiple elements. Many of these are common to most or all pages, such as the sidebar and Page Title.

The main content area of a page is the part of the page which that is unique to that page (the part that is not shared with other pages).

So, for a text page, the main content area is the text you write. For a photo page, it is the photo itself.
