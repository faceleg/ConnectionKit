Giving a pagelet or page a caption

You can give almost any kind of pagelet a caption. This is a short piece of text above or below the main content of the pagelet.

To add a caption to a pagelet:

  1. Select the page containing the pagelet in the Site Outline.
  2. Make sure Editing Markers () are visible by clicking the "Show Markers" item in the toolbar if necessary.
  3. Click the Editing Marker above or below the pagelet's main content.
  4. Enter your caption.
  5. Click outside of the pagelet to end editing.

Some page types (e.g. Photo or Video) also allow you to add a caption.

To add a caption to a suitable page:

  1. Select the page in the Site Outline.
  2. Make sure Editing Markers () are visible by clicking the "Show Markers" item in the toolbar if necessary.
  3. Click the Editing Marker to begin editing the caption.
  4. Enter your text.
  5. Click outside of the text to end editing.
