Text alignment

Sandvox allows you to set the alignment of most items of text in a site. You can select from:

  • align left
  • center
  • align right
  • justify

To set the alignment of some text:

  1. Click the text to begin editing it.
  2. Open the "Format â†’ Alignment" menu and choose an alignment.

Note that the alignment of some text (mainly certain titles) may be part of the site design and so cannot be changed.
