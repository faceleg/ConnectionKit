Site Inspector

The Site Inspector is one segment of the Inspector, used to adjust properties and settings of the whole of a site.

To open the Site Inspector:

  1. Click the "Inspector" item in the toolbar or choose "Show Inspector" from the Window menu.

  2. Select the "Site" segment.

The Site Inspector is split into three tabs:

Properties
General site properties.
Media
Settings for the media components of the site.
Google
Only available in Sandvox Pro. Allows the addition of custom code to the site.
