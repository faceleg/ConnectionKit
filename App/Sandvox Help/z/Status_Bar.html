Status Bar

The Status Bar appears at the bottom of every document window. It displays informational text about the item the mouse is hovering over. So, if you hover over a link to the Home Page, the Status Bar will display "Go to Home." Or, if you hover over an Editing Marker (), the text will read "Click on + to insert an element."

To show or hide the Status Bar:

  1. Open the View menu.
  2. Choose "Show Status Bar" or "Hide Status Bar."
