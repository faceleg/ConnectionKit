Design Chooser

The Design Chooser allows you to browse and select a design for your site. It is located across the top of document windows, just under the toolbar:

Ways to hide or show the Design Chooser:

  • Click the "Designs" item in the toolbar.
  • Choose "Show Designs" or "Hide Designs" from the View menu.

To select the design for a site, simply click a design in the Design Chooser.

As you hover over a design, its name will appear, and, if applicable, the name of its contributor. If the design is from a third party contributor, clicking the name will take you to their website.

To navigate the Design Chooser you use the arrows located to the left and right. You can also use these keys when clicking to gain some additional behavior:

  • Hold down command and click to move to the start or end of all available designs.
  • Hold down shift and click to view the scrolling effect in slow motion.
  • Hold down option to see the opposite animation effect to normal for your computer.
