Page width

The width of a page is determined by the design the site uses. Most designs have a fixed page width. This means that pages are the same width, regardless of the size visitors set their browser window to.

However, a few designs don't have a fixed width; they resize to fit the width of browser windows. For some good examples of these, you can try downloading the Bonus Designs Pack from the Karelia website; it is free when you sign up to the mailing list.
