Banner

Some site designs feature a banner; a large image across the top of the page. For example, the Crimson Royale design:

There are three ways that Sandvox designs may handle banners:

  • Those with a rectangular "banner" image, which you can replace;
  • Those without a banner image, initially, but that allow you to add your own;
  • Those that do not support banners.

(If a design does not support a banner image, there is no way to add one. And if the design initially comes with a banner design, there is no way to completely remove the banner; attempting to remove a customized banner will just revert the image back to its initial image.)

Replacing the initial Banner

Sandvox allows you to replace the banner with one of your own choosing.

To set the banner image of a site:

  1. Open the Site Inspector.
  2. Go to the Media tab.
  3. Click the "Choose" button to navigate to the file you wish to set as your banner, OR drag your new banner image to the box labeled "Banner" from the Media Browser, the Finder, or another application.

Reverting or removing your custom banner

To set the banner image of a site to its default image (or remove it, if applicable):

  1. Open the Site Inspector.
  2. Go to the Media tab.
  3. Click the small "X" next to the input field that holds the name of the current banner.

Sizing

Banner images can be a maximum of 800 pixels wide and 300 pixels high.

  • If you try to use an image wider than 800 pixels, Sandvox will shrink it to that width.
  • If the image is taller than 300 pixels (even after shrinking), Sandvox will crop it to be 300 pixels high.


However, the actual space available for the banner varies from design to design:

Dolphin Dance 760 200

Clockwork 760 200
Cathedral 749 144 (265 on the home page)

Sandvox will attempt to fit your supplied banner image to this size as well as it can. The image will be centered horizontally, and then aligned at the top; the excess is cropped. If the result isn't satisfactory, try cropping the image (using an application such as Preview) to a more appropriate size before inserting it.

Banner Image Sources

  • freewebpageheaders.com has a large number of images available for free, as long as your site is for non-commercial use and they are credited.
  • The Masthead Factory provides custom banners. At their checkout you can input the size required to match your Sandvox design.

Of course, you can also use photos of your own. Just be sure to crop them appropriately first.
