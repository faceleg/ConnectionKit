Video introduction

Sandvox features a video introduction to help you get started with creating a website. It is available in two sizes:

  • Standard (640 x 480 pixels)
  • Full (1024 x 768 pixels)

To view the video requires an Internet connection of at least 64 kbps.

To view the Sandvox video introduction:

  1. Open the Help menu and go to "Video Introduction."
  2. Select a video size. You can hold down the option key when clicking a video size to open it in your web browser rather than Quicktime Player.

or:

  1. View the screencast online at the Karelia website.

Note that some older computers may have trouble playing the full-size version.
